Background and places visited

My name is Ismael Ouedraogo. I'm a student at Cleveland Codes. I'm a graduate of Cleveland States and the University of Ouagadougou. I grew up in Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso and went to Cours Marie Curie one of my local high schools. After graduation, I went to the University of Ouagadougou, where I got Bachelor in Environmental Economics and I left Burkina Faso for the United States, I spent my first 2 years in New York, before I moved to Cleveland, where I got my second bachelor's degree in General Economics. I'm from Burkina Faso; a land lock country located in the middle of West Africa.
Burkina Faso has nineteen million people Burkina Faso. The country is dry from October to April and rainy from Mai to September. It's sunny all year long. French is the official language, but there are sixty other languages spoken.

I've visited many countries besides the USA. In fact, I visited Ghana, Nigeria, Benin, and Morocco.
Benin was the first country that I visited, followed by Nigeria, Morocco and the United States. I visited Ghana in December 2018.
The United States is a big country with different varieties of weathers; I visited New York, Florida, and Washington DC.
